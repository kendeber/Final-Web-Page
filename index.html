Welcome to Camp!

Twin Lakes Camp, located in Hillsboro, Indiana, is a great place to have fun and grow. Since 1950 we have welcomed thousands of youth and adults to our camps. Our purpose continues to be that of providing campers with a safe, challenging and enjoyable Christian Camping experience in the perfect environment: God's great outdoors!
