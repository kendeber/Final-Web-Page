Leadership Camp

For students who feel God may be calling them into full-time vocational ministry or who just want to be challenged spiritually on a deeper level.
Activities include Paintball, Challenge Mountain High Ropes Course, Chapel, Boating, Fishing, Disc Golf, Ministry Trip, "The Blob", and more!
Students entering 9th - recent graduates 2019. Fee is $395 per week.
